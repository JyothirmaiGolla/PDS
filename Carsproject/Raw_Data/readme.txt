Car Model: The specific make and model of the vehicle.

Location: Where you can find the car for sale.

Year: The year or edition of the car.

Kilometers Driven: The total number of kilometers the car has been driven.

Fuel Type: The type of fuel the car uses.

Transmission: The type of transmission the car has.

Owner Type: Indicates whether the car is brand new, secondhand, or falls into another category.

Mileage: The manufacturer's stated fuel efficiency in kilometers per liter or kilometers per kilogram.

Engine Displacement: The size of the car's engine in cubic centimeters.

Engine Power: The maximum engine power in brake horsepower (bhp).

Seating Capacity: The number of seats available in the car.

New Car Price: The price of the same car when brand new.

Used Car Price: The current price of the used car in Indian Rupees (in lakhs).
